What We Do

Complete Home Cleaning, Room by Room

Our home cleaning service covers your entire house from entry to exit. Whether it's a 1 BHK or a large villa, our trained professionals follow a strict room-by-room checklist to ensure nothing is missed. We use eco-safe, non-toxic products that are safe for children and pets.

Every visit includes thorough dusting of all surfaces, mopping of all floors, vacuuming of carpets and sofas, cleaning of fans and ceiling fixtures, wiping of windows (interior), and sanitizing high-touch areas like switches and door handles.
